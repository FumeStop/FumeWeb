FumeStop Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1st January 2024

At FumeStop, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect the information collected from users of the FumeStop community, including its associated Discord bots and services.

Information Collection

FumeStop collects and stores user IDs and Discord server IDs to facilitate the functionality of the FumeStop community and associated bots. This information is solely used for providing services and enhancing user experience within the community.

Use of Information

a. Enhancing User Experience: The collected user IDs and Discord server IDs are used to personalize user experience, provide services, and improve the functionality of the FumeStop community.
b. Communication: We may use the provided contact information to respond to inquiries, provide support, or communicate important updates related to the FumeStop community.

Data Security

a. Protection Measures: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect the stored information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
b. Data Retention: We will retain the collected information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law.

Information Sharing

We do not share user information with third-party services, except as required to provide services or as necessary to comply with legal obligations.

User Rights

Users have the right to access their stored information and request its deletion by contacting us via the channels mentioned in Contact Information, as outlined in the Terms of Service.

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Union (EU) or the European Economic Area (EEA), please note that our collection and use of your information are in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have the right to request access to your personal data, request its deletion, and restrict or object to its processing. You may exercise these rights by contacting us via the channels mentioned in Contact Information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

FumeStop is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us to request the deletion of the information.

Contact Information

For any questions, concerns, or inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your information within the FumeStop community, please contact Sn1F3rt (@sn1f3rt) via the community server or via email at contact@fumes.top.
